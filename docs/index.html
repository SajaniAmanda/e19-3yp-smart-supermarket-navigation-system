Welcome to Shop Wise!

A smart indoor navigation system for supermarkets

Welcome to a groundbreaking venture where innovation meets convenience! In this exciting project, our goal is to pioneer the development of an indoor navigation system tailored specifically for supermarkets.

Problem Domain



Our vision is to revolutionize the shopping experience for customers while offering invaluable insights for shop owners. Imagine seamlessly navigating the aisles of a supermarket, effortlessly finding your desired products with a user-friendly interface.Our mission is to transform this vision into reality by blending cutting-edge technology with a customer-centric approach. From enhancing convenience for shoppers to providing actionable data for shop owners, we are poised to create an indoor navigation system that transcends traditional boundaries.

Join us on this transformative journey as we embark on the mission to not just improve, but redefine the supermarket shopping experience. Together, we are set to create a technological marvel that harmonizes the needs of both customers and shop owners, setting new standards in the world of retail innovation. Key Features

In this project we hope to develop an indoor navigation system for supermarkets, aimed at enhancing the shopping experience of customers while providing valuable insights for the shop owners.

Map Navigation

Our smart indoor navigation system offers real-time cart tracking and dynamic map updates, providing customers with an interactive supermarket map while enabling store owners to make data-driven layout and product placement decisions.

Customer

Our smart indoor navigation system enhances the shopping experience through efficient navigation, shopping list creation, effortless checkout, and personalized recommendations. By optimizing paths and guiding customers to find items on their shopping list, it streamlines the shopping process.

Shop Owner

Our system provides an intuitive admin panel for supermarket owners to manage the map, aisle configurations, and product placements. By analyzing customer behavior and preferences, owners can make data-driven decisions on product positioning and personalized recommendations.

High Level System Architecture

Our comprehensive system solution incorporates custom hardware, a mobile app for customers, and a web app for supermarket owners to deliver a seamless and tailored shopping experience.
